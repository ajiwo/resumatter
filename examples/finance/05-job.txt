# Treasury Analyst

## Company: PT Astra International Tbk
## Location: Jakarta Pusat
## Employment Type: Full-time
## Salary Range: Rp 12,000,000 - Rp 18,000,000

### Company Background:
PT Astra International is one of Indonesia's largest conglomerates with diversified business portfolio including automotive, financial services, heavy equipment, agribusiness, infrastructure, and information technology.

### Position Summary:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Treasury Analyst to support our corporate treasury operations. The successful candidate will assist in cash management, foreign exchange operations, and financial risk management activities.

### Key Responsibilities:
- Monitor daily cash positions across multiple bank accounts and subsidiaries
- Prepare cash flow forecasts and liquidity analysis reports
- Execute foreign exchange transactions and monitor FX exposures
- Assist in debt management and banking relationship activities
- Support month-end and year-end treasury reporting processes
- Maintain treasury systems and ensure data accuracy
- Assist in preparation of board reports and regulatory filings
- Support treasury policy implementation and compliance monitoring

### Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field
- 2-3 years of experience in treasury, banking, or corporate finance
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
- Proficient in Microsoft Excel (advanced level including VBA)
- Knowledge of treasury management systems (TMS) preferred
- Understanding of financial instruments (FX, derivatives, bonds)
- Good command of English (both written and spoken)
- Professional certification (CTP, FRM) is an advantage

### Technical Requirements:
- Experience with SAP or similar ERP systems
- Knowledge of Bloomberg or Reuters for market data
- Familiarity with SWIFT messaging and bank connectivity
- Understanding of Indonesian banking regulations and OJK requirements
- Basic knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting

### Personal Attributes:
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- High level of integrity and confidentiality
- Team player with ability to work independently

### Benefits Package:
- Competitive salary with annual performance bonus
- Comprehensive health insurance (employee + family)
- Life insurance and pension fund
- Annual leave and sick leave entitlements
- Professional development and training opportunities
- Employee stock ownership program
- Transportation allowance
